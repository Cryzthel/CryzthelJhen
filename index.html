About Me

Hello! My name is Cryzthel Jhen Quidan, and I am a 3rd-year BSIT student at Western Institute of Technology (WIT). I’m passionate about creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and practical. My main focus is on UI/UX design because I love transforming complex ideas into simple, user-friendly solutions.

My interest in web design started with a fascination for how technology and art combined together. I enjoy bringing ideas to life through clean coding and designing interfaces that are easy to navigate. When I’m not working on projects, I like to keep up with the latest design trends, sketch out new concepts, or explore the outdoors, as nature often inspires my creativity. To me, good design is like having a conversation—it’s about connecting with users and making their experience meaningful. I’m always eager to learn more and improve my skills, and I look forward to creating innovative and impactful digital work.
